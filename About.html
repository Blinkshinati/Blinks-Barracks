About

This site was initally created to compile into a single place various and hard to find British Army training manuals of the 19th century. But, after finishing that I decided to make this a pet project where I could digitise other educational resources like the iterations of Lee rifles as well as to start a blog where I could write freely on various military and general historical topics that interest me.
