British Army Training Manuals
of the Late 18th and 19th Century

Preface:

The cover page images shown on this site are hyperlinks which take you to a place where you can access each document in full.

Each Manual/ Regulation will also have included beside it both the general-issue long-arms used as well as relevant wars and campaigns that occured around the time it was written.

*Please note this is not a comprehensive index, manuals between 1804 and 1847 are difficult to find.
